Dr. Amrit is a physician-turned-data scientist. He is pursuing his MS in computer science at Georgia Tech, USA. He did his MBBS from Maulana Azad medical college(MAMC) and Lok Nayak Hospital, Delhi, India. He believes that the fusion of medicine and artificial intelligence is an essential path to the democratization of health. He participates in Kaggle competitions and currently is Kaggle 4x Expert.

​This blog is on his journey of artificial intelligence in medicine.
